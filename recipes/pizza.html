Pizza

Description

Pizza is a delicious, versatile dish consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with a variety of ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked to perfection, its crispy crust and melty, savory toppings make it a beloved comfort food around the world.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Optional Toppings:
    • Pepperoni
    • Bell Peppers
    • Mushtooms
    • Pineapple
    • Whatever you like!

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil.
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until the cheese melts.
