Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A vanilla milkshake is a creamy, smooth treat made from rich vanilla ice cream, milk, and a touch of sweetness. Blended to perfection, it offers a deliciously indulgent flavor that's both refreshing and comforting with every sip.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
